Fabio Menconi

MSc in Structural Engineering, Politecnico di Milano.

I am particularly interested in the dynamic response of buildings and structures, especially under wind and seismic loading. During my academic path I had the opportunity to deepen these topics, particularly in relation to wind engineering through my MSc thesis, where I developed a computational tool to estimate the wind-induced response of tall buildings.

My portfolio presents selected academic and technical work in structural analysis, seismic design, wind engineering, and BIM-based modelling.
